**Definition: Hermite Polynomial, $H_n$**
:::
::: card-body
Let $n \in \N$. The ___Hermite polynomial___ $H_n \in \Z[x]$ is defined by the following formula:
\[ H_n(x) \coloneqq (-1)^n e^{x^2} \frac{\dd^n}{\dd x^n} e^{-x^2} \]
